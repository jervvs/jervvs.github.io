title: "Runbook Generator"
description: "Turns messy notes into structured step-by-step guides that anyone can follow."
order: 5
tags: ["documentation", "automation"]
---

Every team has tribal knowledge — the things everyone *knows* but nobody has written down. When that knowledge lives only in people's heads, it creates fragile systems and stressful on-call rotations.

This tool takes unstructured notes, past incident write-ups, and scattered documentation, and turns them into structured runbooks with clear decision trees: "if you see X, do Y. If Y doesn't work, escalate to Z."

The goal is to make it easy for anyone to handle common problems, even if they've never seen them before. Good documentation is a force multiplier — it makes the whole team more capable, not just the experts.
